Nick Niziolek - vtol quad plane
-------------------------------


    about:
    Although I am fairly comfortable with both quads and fixed wings, I have yet to design a mix of the two, a VTOL aircraft. I have been interested in the concept for a while, but fairly dissatisfied with the lack of performance you get from the tradeoffs, the performance of interest in this case is maneuverability. Recently I was re-introduced to the blade inductrix specifically it's variant with a small foam wing. Therefore, this project is my attempt to scale that up to a level it probably didn't need to be.
